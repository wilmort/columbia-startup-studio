Findings

  • Page length was hurting fomo_scroller: 50% ‚Üí 75% conversion from trimming alone. Shorter page = less friction for mobile scrollers.
  • Honesty line was load-bearing: Removing 'most apps die after a week. We know that.' crashed journal_graveyard resonance 25pp. Must be restored.
  • Cold-start context was load-bearing: Shortening city_explorer's cold-start from 4 sentences to 1 dropped conversion from 92% to 58%. Punchline alone isn't enough.
  • Specific examples were load-bearing: Removing '8/10 Thai place' dropped taste_curator conversion 8pp.
  • $99/year was an unambiguous win: 'Too expensive' dropped from 27% to 17%. Best price perception ever.
  • Taste analytics specificity landed: 'Neighborhoods, friend comparisons, category pickiness' drove taste_curator to 92% resonance.

Recommendations

  1. Restore the honesty line in the retention section opening.
  2. Restore the specific rating examples as a single compact line in the solution section.
  3. Expand cold-start to 2 sentences (not back to 4). Right-size the context around 'first it's your map.'
  4. Keep the tighter problem section. The merged fomo_scroller paragraph is driving their best results.
  5. Keep annual pricing and taste analytics specificity. Unambiguous wins.
