## 3. Planning Friction

Voting on 3 options - "One friend curates options" pattern

naturally emerges in groups

### 2. Activity Type Gap

She struggles with: - Non-food/non-drink activities - Especially in
winter - "What are we supposed to do?" problem

This is a **boredom gap**, not just discovery.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 3. Choice Friction (Shows)

She enjoys shows when she goes. But: - Hard to pick - Doesn't trust
mainstream taste - Doesn't always align with group preferences

This is a personalization problem.
