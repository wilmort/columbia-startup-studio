## Key Insight

For endurance athletes:

Automation > depth.
Integration > feature richness.
Low friction > comprehensive analytics.

If strength requires significant manual input, it loses to primary sport training demands.

## Strategic Opportunity

Build strength tracking that:

- Integrates with existing wearable ecosystems.
- Minimizes taps and setup.
- Feels embedded, not separate.
- Supports endurance goals without complexity.

The winning system for this athlete is the one that feels effortless.
