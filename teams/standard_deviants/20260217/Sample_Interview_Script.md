**Opening / Context Setting** 
1. Can you tell me about your research?
2. What is the average size of your team for past research projects? 
3. Do you typically employ graduate or undergraduate students on your projects?
4. For your past projects, how have you handled data collection?

**Current Workflow (data lifecycle questions)**
5. For your past projects, how have you handled data collection?
6. How about storage of that data?
7. How about analysis of that data?
8. Can you walk me through what happens between collecting a response and having a "finding" you'd write up?
9. What tools or software are involved at each stage?
10. Are there any steps where you find yourself switching between tools or doing manual work you wish was automated?

**Qualitative Coding Specifically**
11. How do you currently develop and maintain a codebook?
12. When multiple people are coding, how do you check for consistency or resolve disagreements?
13. Have you ever calculated inter-rater reliability? If so, how? If not, why not?
