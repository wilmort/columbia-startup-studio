**Solution concept:** A shared ledger that auto-converts expenses at time-of-payment exchange rates, tracks balances per person, suggests cleanest settlement payments.

#### 2. Pattern Blindness After Solving 200+ Problems
**Problem:** Student solves 200 LeetCode problems but still can't recognize which pattern applies during interviews. No systematic way to review "I've seen this before, what was the trick?"

**Simple solution:** Spaced repetition system for algorithm patterns - App surfaces problems you solved 1 week, 1 month, 3 months ago. Forces you to recall the pattern FIRST, then shows your old solution. Works offline on subway.

**Pain score:** 9/10 - This is THE bottleneck for most students. Memorization â‰  understanding.
