**Creator trust change:** Doesn't even remember who made it

**If unsure about claim:** Nothing really
**Googles from Reels:** No
**Checks comments:** Sometimes to see if it's funny
**Seen Community Notes / fact-check labels:** "What's that?"

**Would use source tool:** "Honestly probably never"
**Auto or click:** Click — "I don't want more stuff on my screen"
**Preferred feature:** "I don't know what any of that means really"
**Credibility indicator changes sharing:** Doesn't really share Reels, so no

**Pain level (1–10):** 2
**Misinformation consequences:** No
**What would cause uninstall:** If it was annoying or slowed things down
